Practical Tailwind + GSAP Tips for Better Microinteractions

Published Sep 2025 • 4 min read

Microinteractions are the small animations that make interfaces feel alive. Here are a few patterns that deliver impact without hurting performance.

1) Keep Durations Short

For most UI elements, aim for 120–300ms. Faster feels snappier and keeps focus on content.

2) Animate Intent, Not Everything

Reserve animations for states and transitions that communicate feedback—hover, active, success, or section entrance.

3) Use Transforms & Opacity

They are GPU-friendly. Avoid layout-affecting properties during animations.
